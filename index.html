Tourist Destinations

Araquio Festival

A colorful celebration depicting the search for the Holy Cross.

Fort Magsaysay Military Reservation

The largest military camp in the Philippines, offering scenic views and historical landmarks.

Freedom Park

A popular place for locals to relax and enjoy open-air activities.

Gabaldon Falls

A majestic waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.

Gen. Luna Statue and Marker

A tribute to the famous revolutionary hero, General Antonio Luna.

Minalungao National Park

A natural wonder with clear rivers and towering limestone formations.

Our Lady of Sorrows Parish Church

An iconic religious landmark with historical and architectural significance.

Pantabangan Dam and Reservoir

A vital water source and a popular destination for water activities.

Philippine Carabao Center

Learn about carabao farming and its importance in Philippine agriculture.

The Dalton Pass

A historic site known for its significance during World War II.
